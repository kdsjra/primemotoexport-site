About PRIMEMOTOEXPORT

Who we are

Exporting motorcycle gear with precision

PRIMEMOTOEXPORT LTD is a UK-registered company specialising in the export of motorcycle heated gear, technical clothing and accessories. We work with trusted UK manufacturers and distributors to provide clean, transparent sourcing for international retailers and workshops.

We focus on small and mid-sized orders where attention to detail and personal communication matter. Our clients value predictable timelines, realistic pricing and export documentation handled professionally.

From your initial enquiry to final delivery, we keep the process straightforward, structured and reliable.
